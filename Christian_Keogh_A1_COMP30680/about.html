about me.

I'm currently a Student pursuing a Computer Science Level 8 H.Dip in University College Dublin. I was formely an animation designer working for Cartoon Saloon and freelancing. Most of what you see here will be animation related but as this website gets updates I will include much more CS projects. This site is the first of those projects.

Why the change? A few reasons; the low prospects in the Animation industry, interest in data starting in covid and the rise of AI like StableDiffusion and Dall-E.

I like to imagine that my version of Peter Thiels 'What opinion do you have that very few people agree with you on' is that i'm against the general feeling in creative circles that these image generating AI's are a bad thing. Everyone is dooming and glooming when the easiest and best solution is to accept and embrace new technology. So here I am.

I currently imagine myself working in FinTech in the future but since I have a design background I'm also looking at Web Development.

Personal Interests
I love history more than anything; and poltics sprout from that love as well. All of the usual male history buff topics I dabble of course (Rome, Alexander, Napoleon etc) but I mostly obsess over early Christanity, early writing and early Islam.
