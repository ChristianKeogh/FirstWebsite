about me.

I'm currently a Student pursuing a Computer Science (Level 8) H.Dip in University College Dublin. I was formely an animation designer working for Cartoon Saloon and freelancing for over 3 years. Most of what you see here will be animation related but as this website gets updates I will include more Software Engineering projects. This site is the first of those projects.

Why the change? A few reasons; the low prospects in the Animation industry, interest in data starting in covid and the rise of AI like StableDiffusion and Dall-E.

I like to imagine that my version of 'What opinion do you have that very few people agree with you on' is that i'm against the general feeling in creative circles that these image generating AI's are a bad thing. Everyone is dooming and glooming when the easiest and best solution is to accept and embrace new technology. I want to be apart of the technology boom.

In December I Interned at the FinTech company RiskSystem in which I helped out in a FrontEnd Web Development role. I currently as a whole see myself working in FinTech/Web Development.
